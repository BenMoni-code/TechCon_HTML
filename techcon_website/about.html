Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has grown into one of the leading global conferences in technology and innovation. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees, tech pioneers, and global companies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, connect technology leaders and enthusiasts, and shape the future of tech through open discussion, collaboration, and hands-on experience.
